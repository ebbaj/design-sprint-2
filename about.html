About us

GoDo Travels is a travel company that focuses more on the user and the experience, rather than the destination. Therefore, you choose exactly what activities and excursions you want to do during the trip, no matter how niche your interest is. Based on your preferences we'll give you suggestions of a travel plan and several relevant destinations through experience packages, which you can customize in order to meet all of your needs. We work with several external airlines and hotel services to make sure that we can recommend routes and accommodations that will suit you.


You can explore our page to get inspired by our most popular experience packages or read about previous trips of GoDo users. Feel free to contact us if you have any questions. Happy travelling!
